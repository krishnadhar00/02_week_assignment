1. bold tag():
    • It is used in to bold the text.
  2. Strong tag():
    • It is used in to bold the text with some strong importance.
  3. italic tag():
    • It is used in to italic the text.
  4. emphasize tag():
    • It is used in to italic the text with some strong importance.
  5. del tag():
    • It is used in to delete the text. Browsers will usually strike a line through deleted text.
  6. small tag():
    • It is used in to small the text.
  7. subscript tag():
    • It is used in to subscript the text.
  8. superscript tag():
    • It is used in to superscript the text.
